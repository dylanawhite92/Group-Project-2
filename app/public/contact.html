How To Use
Our app is a take on Fantasy Basketball where the aim is to jump behind the wheel of the General Manager. To start, we provide you with the Chicago Bulls as your default team, and then allow you to search through our database of NBA players by team name, or by player position (C for Center, F for Forward, G for Guard) and those players will populate the Available Players section of the page.

While building "Your Team", you can build a team of up 12 players under the assumption of the NBA rules that allow 5 players on the court, and up to 7 on the bench. On top of this, you can have up to 3 sub players in the "Inactives section" that can be swapped into your active team of 12.

From there, you can drag and drop available players into the "Your Team" section, adjust the salary cap slider to adjust the difficulty of working under the constraints of a budget, experience the reality of a luxury tax, and compare scores against other teams in the league to see how your team matches up against.
